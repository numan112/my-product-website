No meaningful interactions at standard doses. Zinc and D3 both support immune function through complementary pathways. Take at separate times if taking high-dose zinc.

⚠ Take separately
Calcium Supplements

VORYN ONE already supports calcium metabolism via D3 and K2. Additional calcium supplementation may not be necessary — consult your physician before adding supplemental calcium.

Consider whether you actually need additional calcium before supplementing.
✗ Consult physician
Blood thinners (Warfarin)

Vitamin K2 affects clotting pathways. If you are taking warfarin or other anticoagulants, do not supplement K2 without medical supervision. K2 can alter INR levels.

This is a real interaction. Talk to your doctor first.

The Full Picture

Habits that compound your results.

VORYN ONE addresses nutrient deficiencies and cellular energy. These lifestyle habits address everything else. Together they compound.

Amplify VORYN ONE

01

Morning sunlight exposure

10–20 minutes of direct sunlight within an hour of waking regulates your circadian rhythm — which directly affects sleep quality, cortisol rhythm, and the effectiveness of magnesium for sleep.

02

Resistance training

Strength training upregulates NAD+ consuming enzymes and increases the demand for NMN's precursor role. Exercise and NMN work synergistically on mitochondrial biogenesis.

03

Time-restricted eating

Eating within an 8–10 hour window supports circadian alignment and mitochondrial efficiency — complementing the cellular energy support from NMN and magnesium.

04

Consistent sleep schedule

Magnesium glycinate supports sleep architecture most effectively when combined with a consistent sleep-wake schedule. Irregular sleep undermines the benefits of magnesium supplementation.

What undermines it

01

Chronic alcohol consumption

Alcohol depletes magnesium and impairs NAD+ metabolism directly. Heavy drinking significantly reduces the effectiveness of both NMN and magnesium supplementation.

02

Poor sleep hygiene

Sleep deprivation accelerates NAD+ decline and increases oxidative stress — working against the cellular repair pathways that NMN supports. Supplementation cannot fully compensate for chronic sleep debt.

03

Sedentary lifestyle

Physical inactivity reduces mitochondrial density and efficiency. NMN supports mitochondrial function most effectively in people whose mitochondria are being regularly challenged through movement.

04

Inconsistent supplementation

The single biggest factor limiting results. D3 and K2 require consistent daily intake to maintain optimal serum levels. NMN and magnesium work cumulatively. Sporadic use produces sporadic results.

Common Questions

Frequently asked.

How long before I notice results? +
Different ingredients work on different timelines. Magnesium glycinate effects on sleep quality are often noticed within 1–2 weeks. D3 levels typically normalize within 4–8 weeks of consistent supplementation. NMN's effects on energy and cellular function are more subtle and cumulative — most people notice meaningful changes at 60–90 days. K2 works silently in the background on arterial and bone health — benefits are measured over years, not weeks.
Can I take VORYN ONE on an empty stomach? +
NMN and magnesium glycinate are fine on an empty stomach. However, D3 and K2 are fat-soluble — they require dietary fat for optimal absorption. Taking VORYN ONE without any fat in your meal will reduce how much D3 and K2 you actually absorb. Even a small amount of fat (olive oil, nuts, avocado) is sufficient. We recommend taking with a meal for best results across all four ingredients.
Can I take more than 2 capsules for stronger effects? +
We do not recommend exceeding 2 capsules daily without medical supervision. The doses in VORYN ONE are calibrated to evidence-based levels — they are not token doses. D3 at 5,000 IU is already a therapeutic dose. Taking more does not produce proportionally greater benefit and can push D3 into ranges that require monitoring of serum calcium levels. More is not better with D3 and K2.
Should I get bloodwork done before starting? +
It is not required, but it is a smart move if you are serious about your health. A baseline panel including 25(OH)D (vitamin D), whole blood magnesium, and a metabolic panel gives you data to track progress. Many people discover they are significantly more deficient than they expected. Testing at baseline and again at 90 days shows you exactly what is changing.
Is VORYN ONE suitable for vegetarians and vegans? +
Yes. VORYN ONE uses vegetable cellulose capsules. The D3 is derived from lichen (a vegan source), not lanolin. The K2 MK-7 is derived from natto fermentation. All four ingredients and all excipients are suitable for vegetarians and vegans.
Can women take VORYN ONE? +
Yes. VORYN ONE is formulated for adults aged 35–60 regardless of gender. The ingredients address universal mechanisms of aging — NAD+ decline, magnesium deficiency, vitamin D insufficiency, and calcium dysregulation — that affect men and women equally. Women in perimenopause and post-menopause may find the D3/K2 combination particularly relevant for bone density maintenance. Consult your physician if pregnant or nursing.
