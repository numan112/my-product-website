The Science

Why these four ingredients.

Every ingredient in VORYN ONE was selected on a single criterion: peer-reviewed evidence. No trends. No filler. No marketing science.

01 —
Nicotinamide Mononucleotide
NMN
500mg per serving

NAD+ is the coenzyme your cells use for energy production, DNA repair, and mitochondrial function. It declines significantly with age. NMN is the most efficient precursor for restoring NAD+ to youthful levels, with clinical trials showing measurable increases in blood NAD+ within weeks.

02 —
Magnesium Glycinate
Most bioavailable form
120mg elemental per serving

Magnesium is required for over 300 enzymatic reactions. Glycinate is the most bioavailable form — chelated to glycine for superior absorption without the GI side effects of cheaper forms. Supports deep sleep architecture, muscle recovery, and nerve function.

03 —
Vitamin D3
Cholecalciferol
5,000 IU per serving

Over 40% of adults are deficient. D3 is not merely a vitamin — it functions as a hormone, regulating immune function, bone density, mood, and cardiovascular health. At 5,000 IU, this is a clinically meaningful dose, not the token amount found in most multivitamins.

04 —
Vitamin K2 MK-7
Menaquinone-7
100mcg per serving

K2 is the ingredient most people taking D3 are missing. It activates the proteins that direct calcium to your bones and away from arterial walls. Without K2, high-dose D3 supplementation can accelerate arterial calcification. These two must be taken together.
